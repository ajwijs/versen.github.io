The <a href="http://s2group.cs.vu.nl">Software and Sustainability Research Group</a>&nbsp;of the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam&nbsp;has been awarded a new Erasmus Mundus Joint Master on &quot;<em>Software Engineering for the Green Deal (SE4GreenDeal)</em>&quot;.</p>

<p>The Master is a joint program with Lappeenranta University of Technology (Finland) and the University of L&#39;Aquila (Italy). It will prepare the next generation software professionals to create and manage software solutions that address sustainability goals by design. The program will start in academic year 2021/2022.</p>
